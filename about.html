About Us

Background

AGTE is a Construction Company specializing in General Building, Plumbing Services, Occupational Health and Safety, Supply of Personal Protective Equipment and Civil Works. We offer quality and affordable services to Public and Private Sectors.

AGTE is a family business which was established in 2011 by Mrs. Susan Mauwane and Mr. Tawane Mauwane. It is 100% black-owned and a B-BBEE company.

Vision

We aim to serve with pride and dignity to the best of our ability with a belief to make a difference in our community, constantly strive for 100% service levels and to create job opportunities for women.

Mission

  • To provide the best Construction Services through excellent service and understanding our client’s needs.
  • To be the best Supplier of Personal Protective Equipment.
  • To employ a professional team.
  • To abide by a code of ethics that ensures all of our business is carried out above board.

Values

  • Providing excellent and quality service according to our client’s requirements.
  • Value for money.
  • Respecting our clients.
  • Being accountable/responsible/liable for our work.
  • Working as a team for the better results of our work.
  • Developing trust between the client and service provider.
  • Honesty.
  • Customer care.
